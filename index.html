Good to know

FAQ

Where are shoots available?

We're based in London and shoot across the city. Shoots outside London are available, with travel quoted per job.

What should I wear?

Wear something you feel comfortable and confident in. We're happy to advise on outfits before the shoot.

Can I choose the location?

Yes. We can shoot at a location you have in mind, or suggest spots that suit the look you're after.

How will I receive the photos?

Your edited photos are delivered digitally through a private online gallery or secure download link.

Can you edit my existing photos?

Yes. We offer editing and retouching for photos you already have.
