Privacy policy

This policy explains what information TLMK Studio collects when you contact us through this website, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We keep this short and plain.

What information we collect

When you send an enquiry through the contact form, you may provide:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone or WhatsApp number (optional)
  • The type of shoot you are interested in
  • A preferred date (optional)
  • A location or area
  • Your message

If you contact us on WhatsApp instead, your message and number are handled within WhatsApp under its own privacy terms.

Why we collect it

We use the details you provide only to respond to your enquiry, discuss your shoot, and arrange a booking. We do not use them for marketing without your agreement.

How enquiries are handled

Enquiries are read by TLMK Studio so we can reply to you directly. We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

How long we keep information

We keep enquiry details only for as long as needed to respond and, where a booking goes ahead, to deliver the work. TODO_RETENTION_PERIOD — confirm the retention period the studio wants to apply.

Whether we share information

We do not sell your information and do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. Messages you send through WhatsApp are processed by WhatsApp (Meta) under its own privacy policy.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not set non-essential cookies and does not run analytics or tracking. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts to display the site's typography; no tracking cookies are placed by TLMK Studio.
