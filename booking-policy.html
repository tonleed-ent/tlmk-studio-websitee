Booking policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

This page explains how bookings with TLMK Studio work, from confirming a session to receiving and using your photos.

Booking confirmation

A session is confirmed once we have agreed the date, location, and details, and the deposit has been received. The deposit secures your booking date and is deducted from the final balance.

Deposit and payment

A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking and is deducted from the final balance. The remaining payment is due no later than the shoot date, unless otherwise agreed.

Rescheduling

Sessions can be moved to a new date where possible. Rescheduling is free with at least 48 hours' notice.

Cancellation

If you need to cancel, please let us know as early as you can. Cancellation requires at least 48 hours' notice.

Late arrival

Please arrive on time so we have the full session. Late arrivals may reduce the available shooting time.

Weather and location changes

Outdoor shoots may be affected by weather or access to a location. Weather- affected outdoor shoots may be rescheduled by agreement.

Client no-show

If you do not attend a confirmed session without notice, it is treated as a no-show, and the deposit may be lost.

Photographer cancellation

If TLMK Studio cannot attend, we will let you know as soon as possible. You will receive a full refund of any amount paid, or you can reschedule to a new date.

Image selection and delivery

After the session, selected images are edited and delivered digitally through a private online gallery or secure download link. Delivery is in 3 days for Mini, 5 days for Signature and Car Shoot, and 7 days for Events.

Editing and retouching

Delivered images are edited by hand. Additional or specialised retouching beyond the standard edit can be discussed before or after the session.

RAW files

Only selected, professionally edited photos are delivered. RAW and unedited photos are not included.

Personal and commercial usage

Personal-use rights are included for portrait and couples sessions. Commercial usage — for business, campaign, advertising, or paid promotional work — must be agreed separately.

Use of images by TLMK Studio

We may wish to feature images from your session in the TLMK Studio portfolio or on social media. We do this only with your permission.

Refunds

Refunds for completed photography services are not normally available, though genuine problems will be reviewed individually. Deposits secure the booking date and are deducted from the final balance.

Contact

For any question about a booking, contact TLMK Studio at studio.tlmk@gmail.com, or message us on @tlmkstudio.
